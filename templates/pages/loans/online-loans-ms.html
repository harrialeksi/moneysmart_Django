What are Online Loans?

The borrowing process of online loans is done primarily online, from application, instant approval to funding. You may even be able to get your funds as soon as the same business day. This stands different from most loans that you would apply for through a brick-and-mortar location. Online loans can help you to weather the storm when you’re facing emergency expenses.

Pros of Online Loans

What are the benefits of online loans? Why is the interest rate lower? Is it free from TU? MoneySmart has put together an arsenal of answers for you:

1. Lower borrowing cost

As the name suggests, the entire borrowing process of online loans is done completely online. That is why its operating cost is much lower than its brick-and-mortar counterparts, enabling it to launch loans such as WeLend Personal Loan and UA i-Money Express Online Personal Loan that are characterized by lower or no interest rates and free of any administrative charges.

2. Instant Approval and Disbursement + TU Free

Some online loans don't require proof of income or TU, making the entire application process so much easier, which is ideal for applicants facing emergency expenses. However, it is important to note that these loans usually come with higher interest rates. Therefore, you should think twice before taking out these loans.

3. Online Loan applications are open even during weekend

The entire borrowing process of online loans is not done manually. That’s why it’s not restricted to working hours. Applicants can apply online loans anytime, even during weekends. Many money lenders even offer instant approval and disbursement loans on weekends such as PrimeCredit Fixed Loan.

4. Enjoy Cashback

Enjoy extra cashback when applying loans online. For example, if you apply for UA "NO SHOW" Personal Loan via MoneySmart before 31 Aug 2021, you can enjoy up to HK$5,000 Welcome Cash Reward + Extra Nintendo Switch Lite or HK$1,500 supermarket voucher.
